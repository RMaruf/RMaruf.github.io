Nelson Mandela

Biography of Nelson Mandela

Nelson Mandela was the first Black president of South Africa, elected after time in prison for his anti-apartheid work. He won the Nobel Prize in 1993.

Education of Nelson Mandela

Nelson Mandela began at a missionary school near Qunu.

under the prevalent British educational system
and that’s where his teacher changed his tribal name Rohilahla to Nelson.

Why did Nelson Mandela go to Prison

Nelson Mandela went to prison because he opposed South Africa’s apartheid laws.

When did Nelson Mandela die

Nelson Mandela died on December 5, 2013, in Johannesburg. He was 95 years old. After his death was announced,
his life was remembered and celebrated in South Africa as well as around the world.
